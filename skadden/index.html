Skadden LLP

About:
Skadden LLP is a law firm which is also known as Skadden Arps, it was founded in 1948. The full name is Skadden, Arps, Slate, Meagher & Flom LLP and Affiliates but publicly it is known as Skadden. The headquarters of company is located in New York City United States. The worldwide operations are managed with 22 offices in America, Asia, and Europe. There are around 1,700 attorneys on the panel, worldwide working with Skadden Arps. The practice areas are wide ranging, however, prime areas include regulatory, litigation, and transactions.
