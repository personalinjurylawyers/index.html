Opinion – Law Curt http://lawcurt.com/ Tue, 25 Jun 2019 23:56:15 +0000 en-US hourly 1 https://wordpress.org/?v=5.2.2 https://lawcurt.com/contents/data/2019/06/cropped-Favicon-32x32.jpg Opinion – Law Curt http://lawcurt.com/ 32 32 How billable hours eating the trust levels between clients and their law firms https://lawcurt.com/billable-hours-eating-trust/ Mon, 24 Jun 2019 02:38:23 +0000 https://lawcurt.com/?p=471 Read More »How billable hours eating the trust levels between clients and their law firms]]> Billable hours are seen in the debate over past few years including some popular scandals and lawsuits including big firms like DLA Piper over legal billing. Typically such disputes and claims clients either refuse to pay the bill or law firms are accused of unnecessary work.

The number of such disputes is increasing thus contributing a suspicious environment between lawyer and client relationship. Mostly, large companies have huge overheads and they have to charge higher fees to cover their expense. Therefore, clients are willing to pay higher fees in return for quality service and deep representation skills.

Each new dispute periodically contributes to hourly billing debate and consequently discussions for a change. But over the years such cases erupt and then people forget or they can do nothing about to change it. A respectable and highly experienced attorney at a reputable firm shares his opinion on an occasion where he says;

Big Law firms inherit much deeper problems within their business model. Moreover, he adds that just like former scandals on billing, the current disputes will also pass away and hourly billing will live.

It’s not just a billable-hour system which is leading an environment of mistrust in community and law firms rather it involves a complete system. Where reputable law firms offer rewards to partners and associates for working at high hours. There are multiple other things which now become an operating culture at our law firms.

The change to all this can only occur if courts announce any decision to back away from billable hour policies, clients strictly start to force for alternative way of billing, market leaders (law firms) starts to behave in a manner where they quit looking to increase short-term revenues and avoid any such directions for partners also.

Furthermore, if we look into this issue from a community perspective then hourly billing contributes towards the higher legal cost. This approach of billing makes the law firms at the safe side where all the risk of achieving goals or not lies with the client. However, some instance are available where law firms share the benefits with clients in case they win over claims and in case they lose there are no charges.

In the long run, such scandals just contribute to making the image of lawyers and law firms a bit suspicious. Moreover, it also strengthens the perception among the general community that lawyers overbill their clients typically and they must ask questions on invoices and their work.

All such scenarios empower a client to think about methods and controls to scrutinize the billing by using a number of means. However, all such outcry on billable hours just undermines the trust-ability of lawyers among the general public.

]]> Do online surveillance laws contributing tradeoff to free speech and privacy? https://lawcurt.com/online-surveillance-law/ https://lawcurt.com/online-surveillance-law/#respond Sun, 16 Jun 2019 10:17:03 +0000 https://lawcurt.com/?p=72 Read More »Do online surveillance laws contributing tradeoff to free speech and privacy?]]> A balance between state power against individual rights in the world of the internet is necessary to keep everything at the proper place. Every state has set up its online snooping laws to deal with cases and to support the investigatory powers against the citizen’s rights. The objective is to improve the transparency in the whole process and more importantly legal certainty.

Online surveillance laws typically aim to regulate online activity, more importantly, the role of social media is in focus under such practices. It’s a common discussion among common people that such laws and policies oppose the basic rights of privacy and free speech. But in reality, such laws tend to cover up both positive and negative sides of basic rights.

For instance, if we think about the recent data protection laws (GDPR), then you will see it attempts to protect citizens in term of information breach or theft by using illegal techniques or means. On the other side, this data protection also helps law enforcement agencies to take down, block, or remove websites or apps that are violating and breaching consumer privacy.

In all over the world, most countries even third world countries including India do also have rules in place to monitor and regulate the information passing over via the internet or other channels. However, each country deals with different aspects of complexities with unlike means. Therefore, one has to consult a professional before inducing any type of judgment or taking any decision.

In 2013, it goes public that the government of the USA and UK are collecting consumer data. Furthermore, the claims were that the USA is operating PRISM and UPSTREAM while UK is operating TEMPORA for data collection.

The aim of such programs is to anticipate the future needs and understanding of current conditions. The data collected under such programs is increasing its size by 10x every 5 years. Moreover, the mobile sensors data, social media pulses, and other user behavior are also growing.

Online surveillance laws or online snooping laws always initiate a debate in the community where some people are in the right of the bill while some are against it. But in all cases, the government is found contending for cybercrime, terrorism, data breach, and darknet risks and most importantly crimes associated with these activities.

As stated earlier most states have such regulations and laws in place and it’s not just the USA or UK. Such laws require the service providers to collect and intercept the data and store it separate for the potential probability of use in any upcoming crime investigation. Moreover, all businesses and consumers then have to follow the rules set by such regulations and especially business owners have to fulfill all mentioned requirements for smooth operations.
