This CRM asserts to be simpler for both clients and law firms while offering all the essential features. The strategic integration with external apps helps you to enable productivity and efficient management of your law firm.

A specialty feature ‘ParaLegal Support’ is available with which you can get support from experienced advisors. This great feature helps a lot when you need extra assistance on any specific case or any other thing.

SimpleLaw Features, Pricing & Overview

To start-up with SimpleLaw application at first one has to create an Attorney profile. Try to add more details in the profile as it will help your clients to better understand you and your abilities also.

Attorney & Client Connect

A section is available for clients and attorney to connect with each other and clients can ask questions, lawyers can engage with potential clients, share information about the case which then can be used in future with automatic algorithms.

Besides that platform, security is another thing which helps to make your data and chat securely. For that purpose latest encryption technology is used for both incoming and outgoing data requests. Moreover, a scanning method is active 24/7 for malware, phishing, viruses and another type of attacks.

The cloud hosting feature allows you to connect your account from multiple devices. This helps you to get all the information from SimpleLaw while you are on the Go. Another feature in this section is available ‘client referrals’ which allows your clients to refer anyone towards you.

Document Management

Document management features help you to organize everything according to your needs. Manage documents with viewing rights, control storage option, sync with DropBox and multiple other platforms. An attorney can also mark a document as ‘Private’ to make it invisible for all other users. You can store your documents by case, area of practice, by client names, or any other approach as you like. The export option allows attorneys to move any specific or a set of documents to a USB, or to Cloud storage.

Billing & Payments

The application works perfectly with all types of billing models including hourly rates, fixed, and contingency fees. You can integrate with QuickBooks to simply transfer things while decreasing the workload for accountancy and taxation. Get alerts when a client pays an invoice, create custom invoice formats, and a built-in system to check all pending payments and invoices.

Calendar management

This feature helps a lot to manage your daily routine and workload so that you are always in the know for daily tasks. ‘Court Calendaring’ section allows making reminders for court requirements. For that purpose integration with ‘Calendar Rules’ you just have to make an account with it and sync it with SimpleLaw.

Furthermore, you can also limit view-ability for a specific calendar event to one client, or multiple clients. In addition to that, you can also create events to share with your whole team. Since you are the admin of the SimpleLaw you will see everything in all sections of the software.

Client Portal

A special client portal is available for your clients where they can access their case information individually. Clients can see documents, calendars, notes, and payments details for their lawsuit. This not only helps to empower clients but also helps to build trust in the relationship.

Lastly, security is everything when it comes to winning the trust both for clients and for attorneys also. Multiple tools are used to ensure the security and protection of the whole environment. As the software is built by attorneys so, SimpleLaw team knows well how essential it is to win the trust and confidence of clients.

Paralegal Support

Get solutions to your problems from within the SimpleLaw platform while requesting for support and selecting the type of expertise required. Moreover, this support is available to SimpleLaw application users only as an exceptional service or perk.

No matter you require support for one case, document or anything else, a qualified paralegal team is always ready to help you. Moreover, flexible schedule ensures that you get the required level of an expert at any time of the day or week.

With a wide range of expertise, the vetted team of professionals is able to offer support for all types of practice areas. Paying your Paralegal is easy and you can do that from within software using PayPal. You can also track your paralegal costs and compare it with any specific or multiple cases to determine the profitability of any specific practice area.
