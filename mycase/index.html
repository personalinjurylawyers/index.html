MyCase law practice management software review & alternatives

admin — May 26, 2019 in Reviews • add comment

MyCase is a productivity tool for law-firms and allows an entity to integrate its activity for multiple purposes. As a lawsuit management software provider prime focus of MyCase is to ensure the user experience and product stability.

With this application, you can manage all your case details at one place including messages, documents, and contacts. All such features enable anyone to become an organized person and access everything with a single tap. The features collectively intend to improve the efficiency and even scale of your business.

MyCase Features & Pricing

Here are some of the prominent features of MyCase application which may help to leverage the efficiency for law firms along with other benefits.

Billing

This section makes the payment collection easier with time tracking to charge your clients only hours you work for them. Moreover, you can also use different formats for invoicing to send your clients.

Invoicingfeature of billing and here you can get additional features. One can automate the invoice creation and it will use your working hours entry and applicable rate for each lawsuit. Besides all above it can also send reminders and alerts for pending and outstanding payments.

Different payment methods including credit card and eCheck are available for the convenience of clients. No hidden fees are charged on credit card or eCheck payments. Moreover, it automatically does the accounting work for receiving payments in your MyCase profile.

Case Management

This feature helps to keep track of every important thing for a specific case to enable the organization and streamline your workload. The document management helps you to organize multiple items among custom folders under a single client or case. In addition to all above, integration with Dropbox is an awesome feature where it allows syncing documents between Dropbox and MyCase.

In addition to all above the documents are stored in a secure environment within MyCase using 256-bit AES encryption. The best in class technology ensures that your documents and information never compromised and nor is your reputation.

The calendar section on the other side allows you to get alerts about deadlines and reminders for upcoming events. One can also integrate this feature with Google Calendars or Microsoft Calendars. If you have a team and want to create a common calendar for your law firm or team then the synchronization feature allows you to do that.

Reporting section allows creating reports on multiple segments including accounts receivables, time & expense, financial performance, and more.

Automation is another excellent feature which allows you to automate multiple tasks with MyCase for doing repetitive tasks. In addition to that, the customization feature allows you to tailor your needs and type of work.

Other features

Some other features help to enhance the scale of work, more specifically to improve customer experience. Client communication tool helps to communicate, share files and send invoices. If you get caught in any issue and find it difficult to deal with then MyCase live chat and knowledge base can help a lot. The lead management platform allows tracking, converting, and managing all potential clients. Lastly, the security strength available with MyCase is maintained at bank-grade by using the latest available technologies.
