PracticePanther law practice management software review & alternatives

admin — May 26, 2019 in Reviews • add comment

PracticePanther is basically law firm management software which offers user-friendly interface and integration with multiple useful apps via Zapier and other APIs. The 30-day Panther Promise offer allows checking and using the features of the product while comparing with available options. In addition to that, if you cancel the subscription with 30-days, no questions will be asked and your money will be returned.

PracticePanther Features

The customization at multiple instances allows tailoring your law CRM application according to your needs. Here are some of the key features an overview of each function available with PracticePanther interface.

Legal CRM

The most basic and first to introduce feature under this section is the management of contacts and related matters. You can save each and every detail for your single client including name, number, events, payments, notes, tasks, email, and even home address.

Tag feature allows group-up multiple clients under one section to divide the clients into multiple practice areas. Never waste your time to see the progress of a specific client, just use ‘Follow’ on a specific client and get alerts about upcoming events, payments, invoice due and other things in real time.

You can keep track of payments and even setup retainer budget, get alerts when funds get low. As a result, PracticePanther helps you to avoid work for free, connect with the client to top-up the account before you start work again.

Avoid any bar complaints by increasing collaboration with clients using the communication section by sending messages, emails, and even call logs also. The Workflow automation on the other side helps to save your time in future. Just create a list of tasks and events then apply the template of workflow on it then enjoy the automation of your work saving time and efforts.

Delegation with coworkers allows you to monitor all the tasks assigned to your team with details of completed, in-progress, and unsound marked as overdue. Get a real-time alert whenever a team member completes a task. With the help of email and SMS reminders, you will never forget your upcoming event or task on your list. A daily email will hit your inbox early in the morning at 6 AM detailing your agenda for the whole day including tasks, events, and other important things including deadlines for anything on the list.

In addition to all above document management feature allows you to save important files both online and offline. The generate documents feature allows you instantly fill-up client details in legal documents automatically while saving your time and energies. In addition to that, the Sync feature with Dropbox allows you to view documents even on your mobile while on the go. Integrate Practice Panther software application with your website by using the Intake forms feature. You have to create a specific form in Practice-Panther and then embed that on your website, as a result, all the information goes into practice another. This later-on can be used to generate multiple documents automatically.

Billing and Invoicing

This law CRM software is capable of handling all expenses, invoices, and payment types. It can charge an hourly, flat, and even on a contingency basis. In addition to the above, you can customize your invoice theme and add your logo and colors. The payment methods including PayPal, LawPay, Stripe, Authorize.net, and credit cards are supported with Practice Panther billing system. Moreover, to cater all countries internationally it supports multiple currencies and languages along with relevant GST or VAT integration.

With the time tracking feature you can keep track of every minute you spend on a specific client case. Moreover, the other billable expenses can also be included along with a snapshot of receipt. Shifting between multiple devices while working on a lawsuit doesn’t affect your time tracking, you can start on a laptop and end on a phone seamlessly. Generate weekly or monthly billing reports for performance and analysis purpose.

When using credit cards Practice Panther don’t charge any hidden fees, the customer will be charged standard transaction fees which can vary with a banking institution, location, etc.

Communication section

The separate client interface provides your customers with an extra level of trust and confidence in all the procedures. You can share invoices, balances, payments, share files, schedule meetings and more. Send notifications to clients and your team about upcoming or schedule meetings. Receive notifications from your clients when they send payments when receive and see invoices etc.

Generate multiple reports to analyze the revenue generated and compare performance with former months. The trust accounting compliance helps a law firm to get fearless from an audit. All these features help to maintain stability and grow your business in the coming future.

External Integration

Email and calendar integration feature allows the law firms to collect all important things on one central point. You can share the calendar and emails with your team for collaboration purpose. Your legal calendars can be SYNC with all calendar apps on your mobile phone including Google Calendar, Outlook Exchange, iCal, etc. On the other side, you can also add court rules in your calendar to get updates for any upcoming deadlines.

Sync email accounts with Practice Panther including Gmail, Hotmail, Office 365, Yahoo, Outlook and multiple other platforms. PracticePanther mobile apps allow you to use your mobile phone as your office desk, access all the information including clients, contacts, documents, billing, calendars, etc. with just a few taps.

To automate the case management multiple external integrations are available including LawMatics, Jubilee, DocketWise, Zapier, LawPay, Apptoto, Quickbooks, concierge, Name Warden, and Record Grabber. All of these integrations exclude the names which we already discussed in any section above.
