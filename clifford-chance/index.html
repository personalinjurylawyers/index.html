Clifford Chance LLP

About:
Clifford Chance is one of the largest law firms operating internationally. This LLP Company was founded in 1987 after a merger. Currently, headquarters of Clifford Chance LLP are located in London, United Kingdom. According to number of lawyers and revenue generated it holds the position among top 10 law firms of world. Moreover, Clifford Chance LLP operates across 23 countries worldwide with over 36 offices at different locations. The prime practice areas which Clifford Chance covers include Banking and finance, Corporate and M&A, Capital markets, Litigation & Dispute, Real estate, Resolution, Tax, employment, and pensions.
