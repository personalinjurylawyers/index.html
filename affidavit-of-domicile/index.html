Affidavit of domicile – sample form and filling method

  •  admin

An affidavit of domicile is typically utilized after someone is dead to find their residence place. It is a legal document which is typically created by the estate executor or financial brokers with the purpose of ownership transfer.

Affidavit of domicile is must when an estate ownership transfer includes bonds, stocks and other securities. Otherwise, the ownership will not be transferred if you don’t have an affidavit of domicile. In other situations sometimes banks also require the beneficiaries to submit an affidavit of domicile for ownership transfer.
